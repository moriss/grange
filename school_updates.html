They've Arrived!

Amalia Marrocco trained at Dance for All at the Theatre School and is a fully qualified Royal Academy of Dance registered teacher with extensive teaching experience acquired over the past years. Amalia established the Grange School of Ballet (Edinburgh) in 2008.

In addition to tutoring students through the RAD examination system, her aim is to promote enjoyment in dance through non-syllabus work in parallel to the students core class studies. These classes serve to expand the students repertoire of dance and encourage them to fulfill their potential.

Nikki Grant

Nikki Grant trained at the Dance School of Scotland and the Royal Ballet School in London where she qualified as a Royal Academy of Dance teacher. She also holds teacher training status and has 20 years of dance teaching experience which includes teacher training and mentoring new dance teachers.

Nikki teaches those dancers who have completed all their graded examinations and who go on to vocational training from Intermediate Foundation to Advanced Level 2. These pupils may wish to go on to study dance seriously as a career choice and are given all the advice and encouragement to help them achieve their goal.
