Ballet Teachers

Amalia Nikki

Amalia Marrocco trained at Dance for All at the Theatre School and is a fully qualified Royal Academy of Dance registered teacher with extensive teaching experience acquired over the past years. Amalia established the Grange School of Ballet (Edinburgh) in 2008.

In addition to tutoring students through the RAD examination system, her aim is to promote enjoyment in dance through non-syllabus work in parallel to the students core class studies. These classes serve to expand the students repertoire of dance and encourage them to fulfill their potential.
