Hansel and Gretel 2024

Tickets for the 2024 Production of Hansel and Gretel are now available to purchase.

All pupils will be performing on both days.

Unlike previous years we are now using StageStubs to manage our ticket sales. Once purchased, the tickets are downloaded and can be scanned on entry to the auditorium.

Buying tickets is straight forward via the Stagestubs website.

The link above should take you directly to the ticket purchase page for this event. If you are having trouble accessing via the link, simply visit the stage stubs website at stagestubs.com.
You then click on 'Buy Tickets' and use the search box to enter 'Hansel and Gretel'. Use the drop down feature to find the Grange School of Ballet's production.

Please ensure you are buying the correct ticket (adult or child) for the performance you wish to attend, (Saturday 5th or Sunday 6th October).

Thank you and we hope you enjoy the show!
